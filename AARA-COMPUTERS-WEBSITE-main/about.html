Organizational Profile & Our Story

About AARA Computers

AARA Computers, Chennai is a trusted Computer Training Institute and IT Service Center located in Vadapalani, with over 15+ years of experience in delivering quality computer education and professional IT services.

We are committed to providing industry-relevant computer training and reliable IT solutions at affordable fees. Our goal is to empower students, entrepreneurs, professionals, homemakers, and self-employed individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in today's digital world.

At AARA Computers, we believe that education is the key to personal and professional growth. We strive to help individuals achieve their career goals by offering practical, job-oriented training programs that enhance employability and technical expertise.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to create opportunities for:

  • School and college students
  • 10th and 12th standard dropouts
  • Homemakers
  • Self-employed individuals
  • Working professionals
  • Aspiring entrepreneurs

We aim to bridge the digital skills gap by providing accessible, high-quality computer and information technology education. Through our training programs, we help individuals develop valuable skills, build successful careers, and contribute to the growth and development of our nation.

Our Vision

To be a leading computer education and IT service provider, recognized for excellence in training, innovation, and customer satisfaction, while empowering individuals with the knowledge and confidence to thrive in the digital era.
